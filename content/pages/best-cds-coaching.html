What is CDS

Combined Defence Services Exam

CDS is a UPSC exam conducted twice a year — the gateway to four prestigious military academies for graduates across all disciplines.

🎖️

Four Academy Entries

IMA (Indian Military Academy, Dehradun), INA (Indian Naval Academy, Ezhimala), AFA (Air Force Academy, Hyderabad), and OTA (Officers Training Academy, Chennai). Each leads to a commissioned officer's life in the respective service.

🎓

Who Can Apply

Any graduate can apply for CDS. IMA requires an unmarried male with any degree. INA requires Engineering. AFA requires Physics & Maths at 10+2 level. OTA is open to both men and women — any degree qualifies.

📅

Exam Schedule

CDS I notification in October (exam February) and CDS II notification in May (exam September). Results are declared by UPSC; SSB interviews follow at respective Selection Boards across India.

👩‍✈️

Women in CDS

Women can apply for OTA through CDS — Short Service Commission as an officer in the Indian Army. The exam pattern skips Mathematics for OTA. The Cavalier offers dedicated OTA preparation for women aspirants.

FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about CDS and The Cavalier's coaching programme.

What is the CDS exam pattern? ▾
CDS has three papers: English (100 marks, 120 questions), General Knowledge (100 marks, 120 questions), and Elementary Mathematics (100 marks, 100 questions). Each paper is 2 hours. For OTA entries, Mathematics is not required — only English and GK. SSB interview carries 300 marks for OTA and 900 marks for IMA/INA/AFA.
Can women apply for CDS? ▾
Yes — women can apply for OTA (Officers Training Academy, Chennai) through CDS. OTA offers a Short Service Commission in the Indian Army. Women appear only in English and GK papers (no Maths). The Cavalier has a dedicated OTA batch for women with specific SSB guidance.
What is the difference between IMA, OTA, INA, and AFA? ▾
IMA (Army, permanent commission), INA (Navy, needs Engineering degree, permanent commission), AFA (Air Force, needs Physics+Maths at 10+2, permanent commission), OTA (Army, Short Service Commission, open to men and women). The Cavalier prepares for all four entries under one programme.
How long is CDS coaching at The Cavalier? ▾
We offer a 3-month and 5-month CDS Foundation batch for those with sufficient time before the exam, and a 45-day CDS Crash Course for preparation close to the exam date. All batches include both written preparation and full SSB coaching within the same programme.
Does CDS coaching at The Cavalier include SSB preparation? ▾
Yes — completely integrated. Every CDS batch includes WAT, TAT, SRT, GTO task training, Personal Interview preparation, and conference practice. You do not need a separate SSB institute. The same officers who coach your written exam also run your SSB preparation.
