WAT Golden Rules

Do's and Don'ts in WAT

Every sentence you write reveals your personality. Follow these rules to ensure you always show strong Officer Like Qualities.

✔ Do's — Always Follow
Write a positive, optimistic sentence always
Keep response to 6–8 words
Use the word's first meaning that comes to mind
Show OLQs — leadership, courage, teamwork, patriotism
Ensure grammatically correct and complete sentences
Use words as noun, verb, adverb or adjective freely
✘ Don'ts — Strictly Avoid
Never show fear, anxiety, cowardice or negativity
Do not use he/she or quote names of people
Avoid idioms, proverbs and quotations
No modals — can, could, should, would, might
Avoid imperative sentences (e.g. "Never give up")
Do not repeat themes across all 60 responses
