Why This Matters

Age is the #1 factor in defence entries

NDA & TES — Your First Window

If you're between 16½ and 19½ years, the NDA and TES entries are open. Miss this window and you cannot enter as a direct 10+2 candidate — ever. Act before you turn 19½.

CDS — The Graduate Route

After graduation, you get one shot at IMA (up to 24 years) and a slightly wider window for OTA (up to 25 years). Knowing your remaining attempts is critical to planning your preparation.

AFCAT — Fly Before You're 24

The Flying Branch closes at 24 years. Ground Duty gives you until 26. Many aspirants discover they've missed the Flying window simply because they started preparing too late.
